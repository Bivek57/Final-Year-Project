About Us

We are here for auto enthusiasts.

Car Sansar is an auto website that includes car news, specifications, comparisons, reviews, and a consultancy form for a simple car buying experience, which will be extremely beneficial for people who are about to purchase a new vehicle. There are many such auto websites in Nepal, however Car Sansar would be the first to include a consultancy form for a simple car buying process. Customers can also buy and sell cars online.
Many clients are perplexed when purchasing a new vehicle. They can't make up their minds on which car is better for them. As a result, there will be a form for them to fill out describing their priorities and requirements for their car. It will also include customer's budget, their contact information and the cars they have shortlisted to buy. All of this information will be used to follow up with customers over the phone and assist them in selecting the ideal car for them. Customers can also post their used car pictures, including its details and contact information, after logging into the website. The vehicle will be listed on the used market, and customers who visit the website will be able to buy or sell their vehicle online.
